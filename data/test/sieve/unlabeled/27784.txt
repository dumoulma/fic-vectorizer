Opportunity Description:
This Tender is for an Outline Agreement for the provision of all labour, materials and equipment required to provide for the supply and delivery of GPS Clocks for the Commonwealth Community Recreation Centre, and in other instances, on an if, as and when required basis. The term of the Outline Agreement will be for a period of 1 year. The City will have the option to extend the term, in its sole discretion, for 3 additional 1 year periods.
